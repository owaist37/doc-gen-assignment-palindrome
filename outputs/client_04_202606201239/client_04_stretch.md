## Recommendations

We recommend the following:

To manage your investments effectively, we will pool all available funds together and allocate them toward maximizing your ISA contributions and setting up a new investment account.

The total available pool of funds is £850,000, which includes the completion payment of £850,000 from the sale of James’s business. Since £200,000 of this amount is committed to repaying the bridging loan, the net amount we will consider for investment is £650,000.

Given the ISA annual subscription limit of £20,000 per person for the current tax year, we can contribute a total of £40,000 to ISAs — £20,000 each for both James and Caroline. 

We will allocate £40,000 from the total pool of £650,000 to the ISAs, leaving a remaining balance of £610,000 to be directed toward the new jointly-held investment account.

The allocation is as follows:
- Total available pool: £650,000
- ISAs contributed: £40,000 (£20,000 for each of you)
- Balance for new investment account: £610,000

This strategic allocation will help in achieving your goal of long-term growth while also ensuring the efficient use of your available allowances.
