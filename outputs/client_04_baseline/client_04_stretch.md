## Recommendations

We recommend the following:

In line with our discussions and your objectives for long-term growth with a balanced-to-moderate risk appetite, we recommend the following actions regarding your investments:

1. **Maximise ISA Allowances**: We suggest you both contribute the full ISA allowance for the current tax year into your respective Stocks & Shares ISAs. This will ensure that you are making the most of these tax-efficient wrappers for your investments.

2. **Pension Contributions**: We recommend making contributions up to the maximum permissible limits into both your SIPPs. This not only takes advantage of the tax relief available but also aligns with your long-term goal of replacing James's income after transitioning from full-time work.

3. **Top-Up the Joint General Investment Account (GIA)**: Given the performance of the Holloway joint GIA, we propose adding a portion of the completion proceeds from the sale of your business. This account has demonstrated solid growth, and further investment here will diversify your holdings and help you meet your future aspirations.

4. **Create a New Joint Investment Account**: After setting aside funds for the bridging loan repayment from your completion proceeds, we recommend establishing a new jointly-held investment account. This will allow you both to invest remaining funds more flexibly and strategically, aligning with your growth objectives.

5. **Rebalance Existing Holdings**: As part of tidying up your investments, we propose partially disinvesting from the Holloway joint GIA to rebalance the portfolio. This will help mitigate risk and ensure that your asset allocation remains in line with your agreed risk profile.

By implementing these recommendations, we aim to enhance your portfolio's growth potential while ensuring it remains aligned with your financial goals and risk tolerance. We will outline the specific amounts for each of these actions in the following sections of the report.
