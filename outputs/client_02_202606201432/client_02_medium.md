## Recommendations

We recommend the following:

To proceed with our investment strategy, we will pool the available funds together from the joint General Investment Account (GIA) along with the disinvestment proceeds. The total value of the joint GIA is £40,000, as per the most recent valuation prior to the meeting. We will be disinvesting this full amount to use for contributions to both Stocks & Shares ISAs.

Our net investable total is thus £40,000, which is entirely sourced from the joint General Investment Account proceeds.

Next, we will allocate from this pool to both Stocks & Shares ISAs. For David Clarke, we have an ISA account (ID: H-ISA-D) with full ISA headroom available of £20,000, and for Susan Clarke, her ISA account (ID: H-ISA-S) also has full ISA headroom available of £20,000. We will utilize this headroom to make contributions as follows:

- **Contribution to David Clarke's ISA (ID: H-ISA-D)**: £20,000, using the remaining allowance for the current tax year.
- **Contribution to Susan Clarke's ISA (ID: H-ISA-S)**: £20,000, using the remaining allowance for the current tax year.

After these contributions, we have fully allocated the £40,000 pool with no remaining balance to allocate to any new accounts.

In summary, we will fully disinvest the joint GIA to fund £20,000 contributions to each of the Stocks & Shares ISAs for both David and Susan Clarke. The remaining balance will be confirmed once the disinvestment from the GIA is finalised.
